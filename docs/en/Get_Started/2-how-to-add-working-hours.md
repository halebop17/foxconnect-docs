# Add working hours

**Managing Your Business Availability in Fox Connect**

Fox Connect offers a dedicated interface for controlling when customers can book your services. Go to the booking settings and then to the “Working hours & Days off” tab.

Two key sections are available:

| Section | Purpose | Key Features |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| **Working Hours** | Define the recurring time slots that are open for bookings. | • Set a standard schedule (e.g., 9 AM–5 PM, Monday‑Friday). <br>• These times appear automatically in the customer‑facing booking calendar. |
| **Days Off** | Mark any period during which you will not accept bookings. | • Specify holidays, special closures, or other breaks (e.g., a weekend retreat). <br>• The system blocks all slots within these dates so customers cannot book them. |

![Add working hours](../../assets/images/add-working-hours.png "Add Working Hours")

### How It Works
1. **General Business Hours**  
   - Enter the start and end times for each day of the week. These defaults are used whenever a customer selects a date that falls within your standard schedule.  
   - The booking interface will only display time slots that match these hours, preventing overbooking outside of your operating window. 

2. **Special Days Off**  
   - Add any date range that should be considered closed. This could be a national holiday, a staff vacation period, or an unplanned shutdown.  
   - Once added, the system automatically removes all potential slots for those dates from the booking calendar, ensuring no appointments can be scheduled during downtime. 

### Practical Tips
- **Consistency Across Services**: If you offer multiple services, you can set a common schedule for all providers or create distinct schedules per service to reflect differing availability.  
- **Future Planning**: Plan holidays and recurring closures well in advance so that customers see accurate availability.  
- **Verification**: After saving changes, preview the booking page to confirm that unavailable times are correctly hidden.

By carefully configuring both sections, you maintain full control over your booking calendar and provide a seamless experience for clients.
